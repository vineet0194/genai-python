Self-attention is a machine learning mechanism that allows a neural network to
weigh the importance of different elements within an input sequence relative to each other. 

Positional Encoding answers: "Where is this token?"
Self-Attention answers: "Which other tokens should I pay attention to in order to understand this token?"

You need both.

Let's use your example
Sentence 1
    I deposited money in the ICICI Bank.
Sentence 2
    We had a picnic near the river bank.

/////////////////////////////

Step 1: Tokenization

Sentence 1:

[I] [deposited] [money] [in] [the] [ICICI] [Bank]

Sentence 2:

[We] [had] [a] [picnic] [near] [the] [river] [bank]

/////////////////////////////

Step 2: Static Embeddings

Initially:

bank
↓
Static embedding

The model still doesn't know whether "bank" means:

🏦 Financial institution
🌊 River bank

/////////////////////////////

Step 3: Positional Encoding

Suppose "bank" is the last token.

The model now knows:

bank

Meaning:
[...]

+

Position:
7

Great.

Now it knows where "bank" is.

But...

Does position tell you which meaning it has?

No.

Whether it's position 7 or position 3 tells you nothing about finance or rivers.

/////////////////////////////

Step 4: Self-Attention

Now the Transformer starts processing the token "bank".

It asks:

"To understand 'bank', which other words in this sentence matter?"

!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Sentence 1
I deposited money in the ICICI Bank.

Attention weights might look like:

Word	        Importance to "Bank"
I	                         ⭐
deposited	        ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
money	            ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
ICICI	            ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
in	                         ⭐
the	                         ⭐
Bank	                 ⭐⭐⭐

The model realizes:

money
ICICI
deposited

are all finance-related.

So the representation of "Bank" shifts toward:

Financial Institution



!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Sentence 2
We had a picnic near the river bank.

Attention now becomes:

Word	        Importance to "bank"
We	                         ⭐
had	                         ⭐
picnic	                ⭐⭐⭐
near	                ⭐⭐⭐
river	            ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
bank	                ⭐⭐⭐

Now the strongest signal is:

river

So "bank" moves toward:

Edge of a river

///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

What is actually happening?

Self-attention lets every token look at every other token and decide:

"Who is important for understanding me?"

So:

bank

doesn't blindly use its dictionary meaning.

It asks:

Should I pay attention to

money?
river?
ICICI?
picnic?

The answer changes based on the sentence.

///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Why "Self" attention?

Because the sentence is attending to itself.
