Multi-head attention runs several self-attention mechanisms in parallel, allowing the model to learn different types of relationships between the same tokens simultaneously, and then combines those perspectives into a richer representation.

Think of it this way:

Self-attention asks: "Which words are important to me?"

Multi-head attention asks: "Can I have multiple people look at this sentence from different perspectives at the same time?"

Let's use your sentence
I deposited money in the ICICI Bank.

Suppose we're trying to understand the token:

Bank
With ONE attention head

The model computes one set of attention weights.

Maybe:

Word	Attention
deposited	35%
money	30%
ICICI	25%
I	5%
in	3%
the	2%

This head concludes:

"Bank is a financial institution."

Great.

But maybe we lost some other useful information.

Multi-head attention

Instead of having one person analyze the sentence...

Imagine having 8 experts.

All of them read the same sentence, but each is free to focus on something different.

Head 1 — Financial meaning
Bank
↑
money
deposited
ICICI

Learns:

This is about banking.

Head 2 — Named entities
Bank
↑
ICICI

Learns:

ICICI is a company/organization.

Head 3 — Grammar
Bank
↑
the

Learns:

"the Bank" is a noun phrase.

Head 4 — Verb-object relationship
deposited
      ↓
     Bank

Learns:

The action "deposited" is associated with "Bank."

Head 5 — Long-distance relationships

Maybe it notices:

I
↓

Bank

Understanding who performed the action.

Every head produces its own understanding.

Then the Transformer combines them.

Instead of getting:

One opinion

you get:

Financial meaning
+
Grammar
+
Entity recognition
+
Verb relationships
+
Long-range context

into one richer representation.
